Chocolate Fudge

Description

Rich, creamy and melt-in-your-mouth chocolate fudge made with just a few pantry staples.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1 (14 oz) can sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 tablespoons butter, cubed
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)
  • Pinch of salt (optional)

Steps

  1. Line an 8Ã—8-inch baking pan with parchment paper.
  2. Melt chocolate chips, condensed milk, and butter over low heat.
  3. Remove from heat, stir in vanilla, pour into pan.
  4. Refrigerate until firm and cut into squares.
